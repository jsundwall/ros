Map

  • Move around Ro's Torv to locate the art pieces
  • Being close to an art pieces will allow you to play a game
  • You can see your achievements from the map

Ping Pong

  • Clear all the blocks to complete a level
  • You have 3 lives
  • The ball can only be hit in the bottom area
  • Not hitting the monkey ball will cost you a life
  • Infinite levels, the more you complete the higher reward

Water log

  • Avoid as many obstacles as you can
  • Each one you avoid grants one point
  • You move the bird with the bottom control panel
  • You only have 1 life.
  • Hitting an obstacle will end the game

Flappy Monkey

  • Avoid as many obstacles as you can
  • Each one you avoid grants one point
  • You move the bird with the bottom control panel
  • You only have 1 life.
  • Hitting an obstacle will end the game

Dress up

  • You must match the image on the right
  • Drag and drop the clothing items
  • Press OK when done
  • If you got it right you pass the level
  • There are infinite levels
